Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for clearance?

You will need your student ID card, clearance form (which can be printed from this portal), and any department-specific documents. Some departments may require additional items like laboratory equipment, library books, or financial receipts.

How long does the clearance process take?

The clearance process typically takes 3-5 working days if all requirements are met. However, it may take longer if there are outstanding issues that need to be resolved. We recommend starting the process at least two weeks before your intended graduation date.

What happens if I have outstanding fees?

You must clear all outstanding fees before your clearance can be approved. Visit the Finance Office to settle any balances. Once paid, bring the receipt to update your clearance status in the system.

Can I complete clearance if I'm not on campus?

For certain departments, you may authorize a representative with a signed letter to complete the clearance on your behalf. However, some departments require your physical presence. Contact the specific department for their requirements.

What if I lost my student ID card?

If you've lost your student ID card, you need to visit the Student Affairs Office to report it and obtain a replacement. There may be a fee for the replacement card.
